About the Event

AWS Community Day Oceania is the inaugural virtual edition of the bi-annual, user-run AWS technical conference. Planned, organized, and delivered by AWS user group community leaders across the region, this online event brings together AWS users to share knowledge and experiences.

All presentations are delivered by fellow AWS users, fostering peer-to-peer learning and community connection—wherever you are.
